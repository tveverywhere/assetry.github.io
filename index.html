The Assetry is an affordable, cloud-based Asset and Rights Management System.

Created specifically for agents, rights managers and TV production and distribution companies, it is built to help you save costs and make money.

The Assetry will help you

  • Create clarity around your assets, availabilities and agreements
  • Know when your licences expire
  • Exploit your rights fully to open up new revenue opportunities
  • Grow your business successfully

Benefits



Make Money

  Identify all your unexploited opportunities
  keep on top of licence expiries
  create a shopping-basket for online sales
  open up your content to new platforms


Save Money

  Cut down on back-room administration
  Reduce the cost of asset distribution
  Automate payments
  Free up your time


Be Organised

  Create a centralised library for your assets and rights
  Access all your content online
  At-a-glance dashboard reporting and avails
  Export your data at any time
